Dedicated Business 2 Business Payments

Accept Payments. Make Pay-outs.

A B2B Payment platform that offers hassle free big ticket transactions from accepting payments to making pay-outs across multiple payment methods.

Our B2B solution powers most business sectors from Automotive and Logistics to Retail and Pharma amongst others.

Features and Benefits

Accept payments the way your prefer

Accept payments your way from Distributors, franchises and any other business affiliate from our large palette of payment offerings including cards, net banking or through ACH/ECS and much more. No more waiting for checks to arrive and clear. Receive big ticket payments directly online and through multiple electronic modes.

Make pay-outs conveniently and securely

Make big or small payouts to anyone including your distributors, franchises, business associates among others, easily without expending additional costs and effort.
